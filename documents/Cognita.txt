The Great Reconciliation: A historic event where humans destroyed AI and closed the interweb, limiting it to the cosmic archives.  The Cognita look back on it as a day of sad rememberance, as they see it as a genocide.

Ascendant Festivals: Annual gatherings where individuals show off their AI integrations, advancements, and innovations. This is where the latest cybernetic enhancements are showcased and traded, and it is popular among the Cognita.
