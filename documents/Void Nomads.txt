The Echo Archives: Somewhere in the vastness lies an age-old library ship, purportedly possessing knowledge about the void event. Its elusive presence tantalizes many, promising profound revelations upon discovery.  It especially interests the Void Nomads.

Voidmarks: A significant number of Void Nomads bear distinct marks on their skin, poignant reminders of the worlds they lost. Although not every Starless individual embraces this emblem of their origin, the majority proudly wear their history.

The Nomad Council: Guiding the Void Nomads is a distributed yet impactful council of venerable elders. While their overarching objective is the pursuit of peace and resettlement, internal sub-factions occasionally harbor more covert intentions.
